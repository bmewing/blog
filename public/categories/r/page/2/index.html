All Subsets Regression What is all subsets regression? It’s a technique for model building which involves taking a set of independent variables (X1..i) and regressing them in sets of (k), where (k) is in ({1,2,\dots,i}), against the response variable (Y). The ‘all’ part of ‘all subsets’ means it’s every combination of (X{1..i}) being drawn (k) at a time. But…why? You’re probably familiar with forward, backward or stepwise model building where terms are added (or removed) from a model one at a time while attempting to maximize or minimize some ‘goodness’ criteria.
